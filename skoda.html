SKODA SLAVIA

Skoda Slavia

The Perfect Fusion of Elegance and Performance

Features

  • Engine: 1.0 TSI (Turbocharged) and 1.5 TSI (Turbocharged) options.
  • Power: 115 HP (1.0 TSI) and 150 HP (1.5 TSI).
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual or 6-speed DSG automatic transmission.
  • Fuel Economy: Excellent fuel efficiency, with an average of 18-20 km/l.
  • Infotainment System: 10-inch touchscreen with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration.
  • Safety Features: Dual airbags, ABS with EBD, ESP, and a 5-star safety rating.
  • Design: Elegant exterior with LED headlights, sharp body lines, and a stylish front grille.
  • Interior: Spacious cabin with premium upholstery, ergonomic seats, and ambient lighting.
  • Comfort: Automatic climate control, keyless entry, and a sunroof.
  • Warranty: 4-year warranty with 24/7 roadside assistance.
